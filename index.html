About Me

I am a researcher at The University of Osaka, working across radiation detection and open science. My work spans BNCT (prompt gamma, neutron monitoring), real‑time gamma spectroscopy and dose estimation, and research data management (RDM) for cross‑disciplinary projects. I hold a Doctor of Philosophy in Engineering (Jan 2025, The University of Osaka).
